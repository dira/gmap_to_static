A bookmarklet that allows to get a static map out of a dynamic Google map, together with its markers, with just a click.

Go to "http://dira.ro/code/gmap_to_static.html":http://dira.ro/code/gmap_to_static.html and drag the bookmarklet to your Bookmarklet Holder Thingie.
